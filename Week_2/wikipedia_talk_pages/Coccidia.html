Coccoidea in Hatnote

[edit]

The term Coccoidea also causes confusion. Suggesting the {{Hatnote}} include superfamily Coccoidea for scale insects.

Do not confuse Coccidia (a subclass of protists) with Coccidioides (a genus of fungi), nor coccidiosis with coccidioidomycosis, nor superfamily Coccoidea for scale insects

The template {{distinguish}} might be a better fit. I welcome input on the best way to word this change. Thank you. - DutchTreat (talk) 20:06, 4 January 2022 (UTC)[reply]
