European Economic Community created in 1951?

[edit]

In the lead, it says: "The FRG was a founding member of the European Economic Community in 1951, while the GDR was a communist Eastern Bloc state and a founding member of the Warsaw Pact."

1) The article about the European Economic Community states that it was created in 1957, not 1951.

2) Isn't it inconsistent to give a specific year for West Germany but not for East Germany? Shouldn't we state the year for when their founding member organization was created for either both or none to be consistent? Maxeto0910 (talk) 00:49, 15 December 2025 (UTC)[reply]

I've removed the year. Nikkimaria (talk) 00:52, 15 December 2025 (UTC)[reply]
