"Ethnic groups" in infobox

[edit]

The Swiss are obviously not an ethnic group. It is odd that the infobox says "ethnic groups: 60% Swiss" yet when you click on the hyperlink (Swiss people) the third paragraph starts with "The Swiss are not a single ethnic group", and in the second paragraph of this Wikipedia article, it says "Swiss nationhood transcends language, ethnicity, and religion".

I understand it is obvious what it is referring to (ancestry, not ethnicity) but I think it's important to be technically correct. This should be changed, just not sure to what - perhaps nationality, as we see on the France article. Changing "ethnic groups" to "ancestry" in the infobox doesn't seem to be an option though.
