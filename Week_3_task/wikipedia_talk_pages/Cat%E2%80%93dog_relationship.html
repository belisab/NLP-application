dog and cat relationship

[edit]

Why are dogs and cats naturally enemies. Lex luga (talk) 04:30, 23 January 2017 (UTC)[reply]

Not always. Cats get along with few other creatures because the cat relationship with most other creatures is predator and prey. This is not so with humans and cats. Humans are often with dogs and cats simultaneously, and they often teach the dog to get along with the cat.

Perhaps at times the dog starts treating cats like dogs, which is good for avoiding trouble for dogs. Although cats are potential prey for dogs, they are not easy prey. Cats can become companions for dogs much as they can become companions for humans. Pbrower2a (talk) 10:42, 27 April 2021 (UTC)[reply]
