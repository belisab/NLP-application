Domestication date discrepancy

[edit]

The article lists duck domestication in 2,000 BC but links to this article as the source, which indicates domestication in 200 BC: https://academic.oup.com/g3journal/article/10/5/1469/6026241. Is this an error (i.e. we should correct the date) or good info but based on a different source (i.e. we should link a different source)? Naturalselections (talk) 20:03, 9 July 2025 (UTC)[reply]

Fixed the typo. Chiswick Chap (talk) 20:49, 9 July 2025 (UTC)[reply]
