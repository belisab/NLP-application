Adam was not male

[edit]

Someone help me here. I read only English, so I wouldn't know about other (original languages). Seems to me that Only Genesis 5:2 clarifies who Adam is i.e. both male and female were NAME/CALLED Adam. Importantly so,this was the naming by God himself. Otherwise, the name Eve was by the male Adam; and most notably, the name Eve is cited to have been given (by the male) after they had sinned (ref. Genesis 3:20). Vusi Dlamini (talk) 08:22, 5 February 2025 (UTC)[reply]

What you are probably referring to is Adam Kadmon, the concept of Adam as perfect human-being and embodiment of both male and female humans? VenusFeuerFalle (talk) 01:22, 14 March 2025 (UTC)[reply]
Adam(AS)and Eve (AS) both are male and female respectively. Both were couple and the first humans to exist. 116.71.189.196 (talk) 04:50, 31 August 2025 (UTC)[reply]
Then how did he populate the world? ~2025-38087-59 (talk) 21:37, 2 December 2025 (UTC)[reply]
