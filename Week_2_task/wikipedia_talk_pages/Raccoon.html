"trash panda" is not a derisive nickname

[edit]

Any raccoon lover will tell you that it's an endearing nickname. It's used frequently by communities that love raccoons - the reddit community r/TrashPandas is 4 times as large as r/Raccoons. This article should be changed to reflect this.  Preceding unsigned comment added by 2A01:4B00:D11F:900:DC84:B4A1:15D9:28C8 (talk) 00:54, 24 August 2025 (UTC)[reply]
