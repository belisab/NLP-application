No way that's a chimp skull

[edit]

There is a photo from Prague of a putative chimp's skull. That is NOT a chimp skull. It's a drill or similar. Chimp skull has more bracycephalic features and more massive mandible. ~2025-34409-31 (talk) 05:07, 15 December 2025 (UTC)[reply]
